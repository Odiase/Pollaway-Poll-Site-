Take A look at Some Features Of  POLLAWAY

Filter Through Polls

Pollaway Provides You with an amazing filtering system, where you can find polls that interest you, by their Titles, The Creator, Categories.
Making The Process Of Finding Polls Easier.

Multiple Choice Selection

Pollaway Gives You The Freedom Of Allowing Users To Vote On More Than One Option On Your Poll( According To Your Preferences Of Course ), seeing as this may come in handy.

No Fraud Voting

Pollaway Has Security measures that have been put in place, so as to prevent users from trying to manipulate poll outcomes by uniquely identifying each voter.

Follow Other Users

Pollaway Allows You Too Follow Other Users Whose Polls Interest You, and sends you notifications when These Users create New Polls.

Receive Notifications On Vote Outcomes

Pollaway Notifies You Instantly The Moment A Poll Is Expired
